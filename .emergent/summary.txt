The user wants to develop a mobile app for the Angolan market. This app will serve as a service marketplace where people can find professionals for various services like electricians, plumbers, masons, painters, IT specialists, accountants, etc. The user emphasizes a unique and modern design and plans to partner with training centers and other institutions.

PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS (based on user clarifications):
- **Authentication**: Support for both Google social login and custom email/password (JWT).
- **User Roles**: Two distinct roles: Clients (who seek services) and Providers (who offer services). A later request added Companies as a third type that can act as both.
- **Core Features (MVP)**:
    - Provider profiles with service categories.
    - Search and filter functionality (by category/location).
    - A review and rating system.
    - In-app chat between clients and providers.
    - A system for requesting and providing quotes (not hourly, but per-job).
    - A service request or job board feature where clients post needs.
- **Verification**: To build trust, both clients and providers must upload a personal photo and a photo of their ID card (Bilhete de Identidade - BI) for verification.
- **Location**: The app should cover all 18 provinces of Angola.
- **Partnerships**: A dedicated section for partner training centers, showcasing certified providers and allowing centers to publish their graduates.
- **Admin Panel**: An administrative backend for the user to control the application.
- **Monetization**: A mechanism for the user to generate revenue from the app.
